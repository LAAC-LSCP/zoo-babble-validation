Column names in that file are:
"","chunk_info","Answer","nvotes","sumvotes","ChildID","filename"



"": row name, identical to chunk_info
chunk_info: concatenation of child ID, chunk ID, onset in tens of seconds, and number of chunk in the LENA segment
Answer: major judgment at the chunk level from zooniverse
nvotes: number of votes for that major judgment
sumvotes: total number of votes for that chunk
ChildID: child ID
filename: chunk ID
